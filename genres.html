The term genre is used quite often around the film industry to categorize the type of film into categories. It's unfair to place a lot of films in a single category. For this reason a lot of films have multiple genres. Sub-genres are identifiable sub-classes within the larger film genre, with their own distinctive subject matter, style, formulas, and iconography. In an attempt to let you understand film genres, the we've made a list that covers many genres. A film's genre will influence the use of filmmaking styles and techniques, such as the use of flashbacks and low-key lighting in film noir, tight framing in horror films credits of Se7en (1995), a film about a serial killer.
