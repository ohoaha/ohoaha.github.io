The filmmaker has the power to evoke a range of emotions from cinema-goers. But, it is not simply enough for them to record actors reciting their line in a set. A movie is a skilful mixture of a range of techniques, from lighting to sound, from set design to costume, that not just tells a story, but creates an immersive cinematic experience. Our Techniques of Cinema guide will provide you with an introduction to the various methods and elements used by filmmakers around the world to create moving (pun intended!) motion pictures of various genres.
